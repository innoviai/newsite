Introduction

Founded in 2018, InnoVI Limited is one of best artificial intelligence (AI) startups in Hong Kong. InnoVi solution specializes in natural language processing (NLP), computer vision, machine learning and big data technologies to help enterprises with the customer services.

The fashion retail solution allow the top leading clients to grow their business faster by understanding the customers’ behavior using machine learning and computer vision techniques. It can greatly enhance the customer experience and customization and help the customers find the right product. Through our system, the Fashion retailers can customized their product adapted to specific requirement.

The smart retail solution can allow the company to solve real problems on fashion, clothes, cosmetics, etc. Our solution provide big data analytics, prediction analysis, machine learning and deep-learning on computer vision and chatbot.
